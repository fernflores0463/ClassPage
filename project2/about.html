About Us

You might be thinking to yourselves, why did we make TigerSauce? We don't know why, point is, we have it now, so might as well enjoy it. Nah, just kidding. We were all thinking one day after watching the game, how come we have a lot of things that are named after the RIT Tigers, but we don't have an official food item for the RIT Tigers? We began brainstorming to come up with idea of a food item that is affordable (unlike RIT's Tuition, like c'mon we're freaking broke) and be ubiquitous. Our solution was a sauce. Next thing you know we are mixing a bunch of stuuf together and BAZINGA, we our very own TigerSauce!
